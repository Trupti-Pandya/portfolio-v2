## About HireAI

**What is HireAI?**
It's Trupti's MSc dissertation — a RAG-powered recruitment assistant that matches candidates to jobs. It ingests CVs and job descriptions, embeds them, and uses retrieval-augmented generation to give intelligent matching and gap analysis. It's evaluated with DeepEval and monitored with Langfuse.

**Is HireAI open source?**
It's her dissertation project — availability depends on university requirements. Ask her directly.

**What did she learn from HireAI?**
The biggest lesson: evaluation is everything. Building a RAG pipeline is straightforward. Building one you can trust — with metrics, test cases, and monitoring — is the actual hard work.
